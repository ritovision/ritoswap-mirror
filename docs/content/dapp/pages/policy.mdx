# Policy Pages Overview

The Terms of Service and Privacy Policy pages lay out the legal ground rules for using RitoSwap. They tell users what they agree to (e.g. on-chain token actions), what data we do—and don’t—collect, and how the blockchain’s immutability affects their information. Both pages also include standard disclaimers, intellectual-property notes, and a support contact.
